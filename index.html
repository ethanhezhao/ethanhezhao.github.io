About Me:

I’m a Ph.D. student in Faculty of Information Technology, Monash University, supervised by Dr. Lan Du and Prof. Wray Buntine, since late 2015. I received my M.Eng from Nanjing University in 2014 and B.Eng from Nankai University in 2011, respectively.

I’m interested in statistical machine learning. My Ph.D. project focuses on Bayesian matrix/tensor factorisations with discrete data (e.g. documents and relational networks). I’m also interested in other related areas such as Bayesian nonparametrics, sampling/inference algorithms, and deep generative models.
