Short Bio

I am a research fellow at National University of Singapore advised by Associate Professor Bingsheng He. Before working in Singapore, I was a research fellow at University of Melbourne from 2015 to 2016, completed my PhD degree at University of Melbourne in 2015, and received a bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from South China University of Technology in 2010.

Areas of Research

Data Mining, High-Performance Computing, and Machine Learning.
