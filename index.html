About Me:

I’m a Ph.D. student in Faculty of Information Technology, Monash University, since late 2015. I’m fortunately working with Lan Du (supervisor), Wray Buntine (supervisor), Piyush Rai, and Mingyuan Zhou.

I received my M.Eng from Nanjing University in 2014 and B.Eng from Nankai University in 2011, respectively. I was a visiting research student in Nanyang Technological University in 2014.

I’m interested in statistical machine learning, especially probabilistic (deep) generative models for discrete data, e.g. texts and social networks.

My thesis panel members are Reza Haffari and Dinh Phung.
