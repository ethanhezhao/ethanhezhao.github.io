I’m interested in statistical machine learning and deep learning.

I obtained my PhD from Monash University in 2019, working with Prof. Wray Buntine and Dr. Lan Du, and also collaborating with Prof. Piyush Rai and Prof. Mingyuan Zhou. Before coming to Monash, I received my M.Eng from Nanjing University in 2014 and B.Eng from Nankai University in 2011, respectively. I was a visiting research student in Nanyang Technological University in 2014.
